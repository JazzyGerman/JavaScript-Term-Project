Farm Games - Readme

Overview:
----------
Farm Games is a collection of simple farm-related games that allow users to engage in various activities. This site is designed to be accessible and easy to navigate to offer a fun experience for users of all ages.

Game Descriptions:
------------------
1. **Build a Farm**: Drag and drop the selected pictures to create your ideal farm. Let your perfect farm come to life!
2. **Horse Jump**: Leap over obstacles with your horse! Try your best not to crash!
3. **Chicken Maze**: Help the chicken find its way back to the coop. Solve the maze and make sure your chicken is back before sundown!

Website Features:
-----------------
- Interactive buttons and easy menu design.
- Responsive site for mobile and desktop devices.
- Simple and fun farm-theme.
